Your ContextGPT profile contains your account information and personal preferences. Access it from the account menu in the top-right corner of your dashboard.

## Accessing your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Go to [ContextGPT](https://ContextGPT.in/) and click **Sign in** to access your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open profile">
    Once signed in, click your avatar or name in the top-right corner, then select **Profile**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile information

### Name

Update your display name:

1. Enter your new name in the name field
2. Click **Save Changes**

Your name appears in:

* Team member lists
* Chat history (when acting as an agent)
* Account settings

### Avatar

Upload a profile picture:

1. Click **Avatar** to select an image from your computer
2. Click **Save Changes** to update your profile
3. To remove your avatar, click **Remove** below your current picture

Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF

### Email address

Your email address is used for:

* Account login
* Billing communications
* Password resets

<Tip>To change your email address, contact [support@ContextGPT.in](mailto:support@ContextGPT.in) with your current and new email addresses.</Tip>
