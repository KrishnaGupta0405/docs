## Week of July 6-12, 2026 (Current)

### New features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Google Sign-In" icon="google">
    You can now sign up and log in with your Google account instead of a password. Just click "Continue with Google" on the login or signup page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Improvements

* **System Status page is live**: Check real-time uptime and incident history at [status.contextgpt.co](https://status.contextgpt.co).
* **Google account management**: Link or unlink Google sign-in from Account Settings, and Google-only accounts can now add a password as a backup login.

### Bug fixes

* Fixed a confusing error when a Google-only account tried to log in with a password instead of using Google Sign-In.
