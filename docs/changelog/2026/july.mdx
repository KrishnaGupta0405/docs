# July 2026

> Updates and improvements for July 2026

## July 2026

### New features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Google Sign-In" icon="google">
    You can now sign up and log in with your Google account instead of a password. Just click "Continue with Google" on the login or signup page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Improvements

* **Connect Google to an existing account**: Already have a ContextGPT account with a password? Link your Google account from Account Settings for faster sign-in.
* **Disconnect Google**: Remove the Google sign-in link from your account at any time from Account Settings, as long as you have a password set so you don't lose access.
* **Set a password after signing up with Google**: Google-only accounts can now add a password from Account Settings, enabling normal email/password login as a backup.

### Bug fixes

* Fixed a confusing error when a Google-only account tried to log in with a password instead of using Google Sign-In.
