ContextGPT is an AI-powered chatbot platform that helps you automate customer support, capture leads, and engage visitors 24/7. Train your chatbot on your website content, documents, and knowledge base to provide instant, accurate responses to customer questions.

## What ContextGPT does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Instant answers" icon="message-bot">
    Answers customer questions instantly using your website, documentation, and knowledge base
  </Card>

  <Card title="Lead capture" icon="address-book">
    Captures and qualifies leads with customizable forms and automated follow-ups
  </Card>

  <Card title="Human handoff" icon="user-headset">
    Escalates to human agents when needed with seamless handoff
  </Card>

  <Card title="Integrations" icon="plug">
    Integrates with your tools including Slack, Messenger, Zendesk, and more
  </Card>

  <Card title="Auto-sync" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Syncs content automatically to keep your chatbot up to date
  </Card>

  <Card title="Multi-channel" icon="globe">
    Deploy across website, messaging platforms, and third-party tools
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-powered conversations" icon="brain">
    ContextGPT uses advanced AI models to understand and respond to customer queries with context-aware answers drawn directly from your content. Configure [instructions and personas](/features/chatbot-instructions-personas) to shape how the chatbot speaks and behaves, and use [chat modes](/features/chat-modes) to control whether the AI responds freely, sticks strictly to trained content, or routes to a human agent.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Rich conversation flows" icon="messages">
    Guide visitors from the very first message. Set up [conversation starters](/features/conversation-starters) to surface common questions upfront, and add [follow-up prompts](/features/conversation-followups) that appear after each AI reply to keep the conversation moving. Use [custom responses](/features/custom-responses) to return exact answers for specific questions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lead generation & user data collection" icon="user-plus">
    Collect visitor information with fully configurable [lead capture forms](/features/lead-collection) before or during a conversation. For richer profiling, [user data collection](/features/user-data-collection) lets you gather structured fields at any point in the chat — then view and export everything from your [leads dashboard](/managing-your-chatbot/leads).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Human handoff" icon="handshake">
    Switch conversations from AI to human agents when needed. [Human support settings](/features/human-support) let you define trigger conditions, availability hours, and the handoff message visitors see. Agents pick up the thread with full conversation history and context already in view.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content & data sources" icon="folder-open">
    Train your chatbot on websites, PDFs, text files, and more from the [content manager](/managing-your-chatbot/adding-content-to-your-chatbot). Connect cloud storage directly via [Google Drive](/data-sources/google-drive), [OneDrive](/data-sources/onedrive), [Notion](/data-sources/notion), [GitHub](/data-sources/github), [Box](/data-sources/box), or [YouTube](/data-sources/youtube). Enable [auto-sync](/features/auto-sync) so your chatbot always reflects the latest version of your content without manual retraining.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Appearance & customization" icon="palette">
    Match your brand exactly with the [appearance editor](/features/appearance) — control colors, fonts, avatar, chat bubble style, and launcher position. Add a [text snippet](/features/text-snippets) for a persistent message above the chat widget. Invite [team members](/features/members) to collaborate, and configure [localization](/api-reference/localization/get-localization-texts) to serve users in their own language.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Integrations & developer tools" icon="plug">
    Deploy your chatbot beyond your website by connecting to [Slack](/integrations/slack), [Messenger](/integrations/messenger), [Google Chat](/integrations/google-chat), [Zendesk](/integrations/zendesk), [Freshdesk](/integrations/freshdesk), [Crisp](/integrations/crisp), [Zoho](/integrations/zoho), or [Zapier](/integrations/zapier). For custom workflows, use the [REST API](/api-reference/getting-started), [webhooks](/developers/webhooks-configuration), or the [JavaScript SDK](/developers/sdk).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
