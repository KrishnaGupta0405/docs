ContextGPT enables businesses of all kinds to automate customer interactions, capture leads, and deliver round-the-clock support. Here are the most common ways teams put it to work.

## Customer support automation

Cut down on support tickets by letting the chatbot handle repetitive questions automatically.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="How it works" icon="gears">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Train on documentation">
        Feed your chatbot your help docs, FAQs, and knowledge base articles
      </Step>

      <Step title="Instant answers">
        Customers get immediate, accurate responses with source references
      </Step>

      <Step title="Escalate complex issues">
        Trickier issues are passed to human agents along with the full conversation history
      </Step>

      <Step title="Seamless handoff">
        Your team can step into any conversation smoothly, right where the chatbot left off
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Benefits" icon="sparkles">
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Automate inquiries" icon="robot">
        Automatically resolve 70%+ of routine support questions
      </Card>

      <Card title="24/7 availability" icon="clock">
        Stay available around the clock, even after business hours
      </Card>

      <Card title="Reduce workload" icon="chart-line-down">
        Lighten your support team's load and speed up response times
      </Card>

      <Card title="Quality assurance" icon="clipboard-check">
        Keep full conversation logs for review and quality checks
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Best for" icon="bullseye">
    <Info>
      Great for SaaS companies, e-commerce stores, service businesses, and any team that maintains a help center or FAQ page.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Lead generation and qualification

Collect visitor details and qualify leads in the background while they explore your site.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="How it works" icon="gears">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Configure forms">
        Set up custom lead capture forms with exactly the fields your team needs
      </Step>

      <Step title="Engage visitors">
        The chatbot starts conversations and gathers information in a natural, low-friction way
      </Step>

      <Step title="Qualify leads">
        Score and qualify leads from their responses, then route them to the right sales rep
      </Step>

      <Step title="Integrate">
        Push leads straight to your CRM or trigger webhook notifications in real time
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Benefits" icon="sparkles">
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="24/7 capture" icon="clock">
        Keep collecting leads day and night, even when your sales team is away
      </Card>

      <Card title="Auto-qualify" icon="user-check">
        Let the chatbot qualify leads on its own based on the conversation
      </Card>

      <Card title="Reduce abandonment" icon="chart-line-up">
        Replace static forms with conversational data collection to reduce drop-offs
      </Card>

      <Card title="Smart routing" icon="route">
        Send qualified leads directly to the right person on your team
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Best for" icon="bullseye">
    <Info>
      A strong fit for B2B companies, agencies, consultancies, and any business running an active sales pipeline.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Product recommendations

Guide customers to the right product or service through a natural back-and-forth conversation.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="How it works" icon="gears">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Train on catalog">
        Load your chatbot with your product catalog, specs, and real-world use cases
      </Step>

      <Step title="Understand needs">
        The chatbot asks the right questions to learn what the customer actually needs
      </Step>

      <Step title="Recommend">
        Suggest the best-fit options based on their requirements and budget
      </Step>

      <Step title="Share resources">
        Link to product pages, comparison charts, and reviews to support the decision
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Benefits" icon="sparkles">
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Faster guidance" icon="gauge-high">
        Help customers find what they're looking for without the back-and-forth
      </Card>

      <Card title="Higher conversions" icon="arrow-trend-up">
        Boost conversion rates by matching customers to the right option
      </Card>

      <Card title="Fewer returns" icon="box-check">
        Cut down on returns by helping customers choose the right fit upfront
      </Card>

      <Card title="Upsell opportunities" icon="chart-line-up">
        Naturally surface upsell and cross-sell opportunities in context
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Best for" icon="bullseye">
    <Info>
      Best suited for e-commerce stores, SaaS businesses with tiered plans, and any company with a broad or complex product catalog.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Multi-language support

Communicate with customers in the language they're most comfortable with.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="How it works" icon="gears">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Configure languages">
        Set up your chatbot with localized content tailored to each market
      </Step>

      <Step title="Detect preference">
        The chatbot automatically detects the user's language or asks upfront
      </Step>

      <Step title="Multi-language support">
        Deliver support across multiple languages from a single chatbot instance
      </Step>

      <Step title="Maintain quality">
        Keep the same level of quality and accuracy across every language
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Benefits" icon="sparkles">
    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Global expansion" icon="globe">

      </Card>

      <Card title="Consistent quality" icon="star">
        Deliver the same quality of support no matter what language a customer uses
      </Card>

      <Card title="Break barriers" icon="language">
        Remove language friction for international customers and improve their experience
      </Card>

      <Card title="Scale globally" icon="chart-network">

    </CardGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Best for" icon="bullseye">
